Philosophy

How they teach

Trinity School delivers a rigorous, student-centered education focused on intellectual curiosity, critical thinking, and character development. Faculty work closely with students to provide personalized support while maintaining high academic standards.

Outcomes

Where students go

Trinity School prepares students for admission to highly selective colleges and universities. The school's college-preparatory curriculum and individualized guidance help students pursue ambitious academic and professional pathways.

How Trinity School Performs

Expert Staff

Faculty Excellence

Experienced educators deliver rigorous academics while maintaining a personalized approach to student development. Small class sizes allow meaningful mentorship and individualized attention.

Student Care

Student Wellbeing

Trinity School prioritizes the academic, social, and emotional wellbeing of students through a supportive environment that encourages confidence, belonging, and personal growth.

Curriculum

Academic Integration

The curriculum combines strong core academics with extensive opportunities in science, mathematics, humanities, arts, and extracurricular activities. Programs are designed to foster inquiry, creativity, and interdisciplinary thinking.
